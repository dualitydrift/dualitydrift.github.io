DUALITY
DRIFT

Duality Drift is currently in development targeting Quest 3, 3S and 2 and will later also release for PCVR and PSVR.

It is a cockpit-based tower defence / strategy with an extraction twist. Build your base, collect crystals and expand. Harvest as many as you can - however, these crystals do not like to be collected. The more you collect, the stronger the resistance will be! If you die, you lose your weapons and collected resources from this round but still progress in research. Between deployments, unlock new weapons, buildings, and upgrades to help you survive longer.
